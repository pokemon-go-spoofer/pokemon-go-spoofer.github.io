--- title: Our team description: We are a team of artists, engineers, and designers who share a love of creativity and technology. --- # About Niantic Niantic, Inc. is an American software development company based in San Francisco, California. The company was founded in 2010 by John Hanke as a startup within Google. Niantic is best known for developing the augmented reality mobile games Ingress and Pokémon Go. ## About Us We are a team of artists, engineers, and designers who share a love of creativity and technology. We believe in using our skills to make the world a better place, and we’re committed to creating experiences that bring people together. We started Niantic in 2010 as an internal startup at Google. Our first project was Field Trip, an Android app that helps you discover the hidden gems around you.
